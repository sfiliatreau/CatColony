About

Upon entering Lake Tallavana, first time visitors or long time residents realize the calm and serenity that surrounds our community. From the slow moving traffic to the golf carts, bicyclists and walkers, this natural park-like setting is tailored to take away the stress of your daily lives and provide a place to enjoy abundant wildlife and scenic beauty.
